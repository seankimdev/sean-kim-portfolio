About Me

We. Why. How.

I'm a mechanical engineer turned web developer in Toronto. I'm passionate about combining the worlds of creativity and logical analysis to build clean web applications for solving problems.

As a former engineering and operations manager from a fast-paced manufacturing environment, I've put strong emphasis on working together as 'We', understanding root causes by asking 'Why', and strategically approaching each smaller problem to solve with 'How'.

In my spare time, I love hiking, camping, travelling, and listening to bossa nova!
